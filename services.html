Diagnostics

You can't fix anything if you don't know what's wrong! We assess the type and severity of your hearing loss with comprehensive exams.

We start by using several different tests. These include a visual exam of the ear to check for physical damage or blockages. Then you'll enter a sound-proof booth to hear some tones to see how much hearing loss there is. Word discrimination (the ability to understand different words) is also tested. We'll also test air and bone conduction to see how well sound is moving through your ear.

After the tests are run, we'll go over the results on the audiogram whick provides a visual representation of what you're hearing.

Hearing Aids

We'll fit you with a set of demonstration hearing aids so you can try out a model to see if you like them. This way you can "try before you buy" to see what type of hearing aid is right for you. Then we'll fit and order you your very own set of hearing aids that are customized to your needs.

Since no two people have the same lifestyle or the same level of hearing loss, no one hearing aid will work for all people right out of the box. We'll look at your needs and customize the hearing aid's settings exclusively for you.

We can customize everything, from the settings for the music you listen to, to the volume so you can hear your grandkids.
